Narada — The Core Intelligence

Not Just Another
AI Chatbot.

Narada is the intelligent layer that sits between your team and every tool they use — making them all accessible through a single, natural conversation.

Most workplace tools exist in silos. You switch between email, calendar, HR portals, project trackers, payroll systems — losing focus with every context shift. Narada eliminates that entirely.

Tell Narada to reschedule your meetings, draft a status update, check your leave balance, approve a team request, pull this week's timesheets — and it does it. Across every tool. In seconds. With your permission at every step.

Narada works with the tools your organization already runs on — Slack, Microsoft Teams, Zoho People, Keka, Darwinbox, Clockify, ClickUp, Workday — whatever your team uses, Narada speaks its language.

Our Vision

"We're not building a feature. We're building the operating system for the modern workplace."
— ChaosCrafted, 2026
01
🌐
Tool-Agnostic by Design
Narada connects to the tools you already use — Slack or Teams, Darwinbox or Workday, Clockify or ClickUp. We build bridges, not walls. Your stack, your choice.
02
🧠
Any AI Model, Your Choice
Claude, GPT, Gemini, Grok — or your own private model. Narada is model-agnostic. The intelligence is pluggable, and you're never locked in to a single provider.
03
🔒
Trust at Every Step
Narada never acts unilaterally on irreversible actions. Approvals, emails, schedule changes — it always asks first. Powerful AI with iron-clad human control.
04
🏢
Built for Real Teams
Not a personal productivity toy. Narada is multi-tenant, role-aware, and org-configurable — purpose-built for companies of 50 to 5,000 employees.
05
🎙️
Voice is Next
"Hey Narada, what's on my plate today?" — A fully voice-enabled AI workplace assistant is the next frontier. We're already building it.
06
🚀
This is Just the Start
Narada is product one. ChaosCrafted is a GenAI studio with a long-term portfolio vision — eliminating workplace friction, one product at a time.
