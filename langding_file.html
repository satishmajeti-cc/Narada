"One conversation to run your whole team — across every tool, in every direction."

Every query is routed to the right specialist. Jira, GitHub, Slack, HR — they all respond in parallel. The answer you get is a synthesis, not a search result.

Why Narada?
Sanskrit  ·  नारद nāra — knowledge  +  da — giver

The sage who moved between worlds.
Who knew everything. Everywhere. Always.

In Hindu mythology, Narada Muni was the divine messenger — the only being who could walk freely through every realm: the courts of gods, the halls of kings, the homes of ordinary people. He carried no agenda. He just connected what needed connecting and surfaced what needed surfacing.

That’s exactly what your AI assistant should do. Not a chatbot you open in a tab. A presence that watches every tool, reads every signal, and acts before you have to ask — then steps back and lets you lead.
